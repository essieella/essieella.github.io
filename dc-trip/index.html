Kimchi Juice

Nov 23, 2018

At Union Market, I met Julia Chon, the creator of Kimchi Juice. Kimchi Juice is a clothing, homeware and print business emblazoned with cute animals “flipping the bird”. Julia was seventeen when she made her business. A year later, she is taking a gap year to explore her art and compete in mural contests.
