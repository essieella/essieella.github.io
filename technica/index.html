Technica

Nov 12, 2018

This year I went to Technica which is the largest all girls coding event in the world. It wasn’t 100% female because there were some male mentors and sponsors. As the wifi wasn’t working, I had little to program with (though I am only a novice, anyways). Scheduled throughout the day were tech talks on a range of topics from exploring tech careers in other fields to machine learning.

Chibird

At around 5pm, an artist techie Jaqueline Chen led a talk about the crossroads of art and technology. Using photoshop and other tools, she created motivating cartoons of cute animals. While she was still a high school student, Jaqueline started a blog under the pseudonym chibird.

One of my favorites was one about a cat who was having a lazy day with the response of “that’s okay.” Her book, Loading Penguin hugs, is only $7.69 and has two sticker sheets in the back!
