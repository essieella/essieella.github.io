IKEA Sleepovers

May 7, 2018

IKEA sleepovers are a new dare campaign in which customers attempt to stay overnight at IKEA. The vlog of two Belgian teenagers, titled two idiots at night in IKEA, went viral resulting in many other attempted sleepovers. These sleepovers are illegal, however it is unknown whether any of these intruders were prosecuted. According to an Ikea UK spokesperson: “We appreciate that people are interested in Ikea and want to create fun experiences, however the safety and security of our co-workers and customers is our highest priority and that’s why we do not allow sleepovers in our stores.”

There are some legal store run sleepovers that I am researching.
